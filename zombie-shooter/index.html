DEAD SECTOR
// SURVIVE THE SWARM //
Headphones recommended · A self-contained browser game
HOW TO PLAY
  • WASD — move the soldier
  • Mouse — aim the flashlight & gun (fires automatically)
  • R — reload early (auto-reloads when empty)
  • Space / Left-click — fire the RPG (when equipped)
  • P / Esc — pause
  • M — mute / unmute
The map is dark — your flashlight cone reveals zombies and walls block the light. You have 100 HP and 100 armor; armor takes damage first and regenerates if you stay safe — but once it fully breaks it won't recharge until you grab an armor core. Survive 5 rounds of escalating hordes, upgrade your weapon each round, then endure the endless round 6. Each kill is +10.
